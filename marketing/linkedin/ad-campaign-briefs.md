## Campaign Management Notes

- **A/B test** each ad set for 2 weeks, then pause the lower-CTR version
- **Frequency cap:** Max 3 impressions per user per 7 days to avoid fatigue
- **Conversion tracking:** Install LinkedIn Insight Tag on all pages; fire conversion event on trial activation (not just signup click)
- **Monthly review:** Assess CPL vs. €80 target; reallocate budget to top performer
- **Pause triggers:** Pause any ad set with CTR <0.3% after 1,000 impressions
